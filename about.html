In the summer of 2017, my wife Lea and I decided to purchase
a National Parks Pass, valid for one year. We started out
going to Zion's National Park, and instantly knew that we
would be using our pass often. Since then, we have been to
a grand total of 15 National Parks in the states of Utah,
Wyoming, Montana, Washington, Oregon, and California. We
have travelled thousands of miles, and have enjoyed all of it.
